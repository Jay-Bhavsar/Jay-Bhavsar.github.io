About Training and Placement Cell

The Placement Cell established with the objective to provide meaningful employment opportunities to its students as well as alumni at all levels matching with their personal and academic profiles. It conducts various Campus Placement Drives/Recruitment Drives/Job Fairs and employability enhancement activities. Their foremost aim is to focus on inviting best companies and aspirational recruiters to the campus, motivate students to make their career in right direction by providing them roadmap.
